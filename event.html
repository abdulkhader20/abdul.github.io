1.Postera (POSTER MAKING CONTEST)

About:

As a part of SPE INDHAN 2023-We invite you to participate in technical poster making contest. The objective behind poster making competition is to bring out the creative expression of students & increases the observation skills, thoughts, awareness and concern of the students. Hence, we are giving a challenge to represent your ideas, thoughts, knowledge through your posters.

GuideLines:

Participants are invited to present a technical poster on topics related to engineering streams

Petroleum Engineering:

1. Advances in Drilling and Completions

2. Advances in Production and Reservoir Engineering

3. Tight Gas Reservoirs

4. Subsea Engineering and Technology

5. HSE in Oil and Gas Industry

6. Flow Assurance

7. Digitiation & Automation in Oil & Gas Industry

8. Climatic change

Chemical Engineering:

1. Process Integration

2. Carbon Sequestration

3. Fuels of the Future

4. Production of Green Chemicals

5. Utilization of plastic and polymetrics.

6. Solar Energy & and Its Application in Chemical Process Industry

7. Industrial Safety and Hazard Management

8. Digitization and Automation in Chemical Process Industry

Rules:

1.Each Participant can post only one Poster.

2. The poster must be made on the A3 size (11.7 * 16.5 inches) sheet with margins (0.5 inches) drawn with black pen/marker.

3. Poster should not contain any personal details like Name, Class, College etc. of the participants.

These details should be mentioned in registration form only.

4. The Topic of poster and theme should be clearly mentioned at the Top Margin of the Poster.

5. Abstract should not be more than 1000 words, formatted in Microsoft Word and single-spaced, using size 12 with Times New Roman Font to explain the topic and content of the poster to be sent our mail id speindhan2k23@gmail.com Irrelevant or non technical posters will leads to disqualification.

Submission Format:

The Poster is to be submitted in online in JPEG Format only. The email id or link is sent to the participants registered email ids entered in the registration form of SPE INDHAN 2023 to submit the poster.

Judgement Criteria:

Judgment will be based on:

1.Relevance to the theme.

2. Message conveyed by the poster.

3. Artistic composition.

4. Creativity.

5. Originality.

6.Effectiveness in communicating the intended message.

Registration guidelines:

Registration of this event Should be made besides basic registration.
Registration fee to participate in the contest is Rs.200/-(2 Participants).

Rewards:

The Winner of the Contest will get an exciting reward along with a JNTUK certificate.
