8.Game of Rocks:

Introduction:

As a part of SPE INDHAN 2023- we invite you to participate in the game of rocks contest. The objective behind the contest is to enhance the thought provoking skills and immense technical knowledge about various types of rocks and minerals. Hence we are giving you a challenge to put your practical knowledge regarding the rock lithology to a test.

Rules:

GuideLines and Rules:

1. Only a single participant is allowed at a time.
2. Time limit of 2 mins 30 seconds will be provided for identification of given rocks.
3. Minimum 7 out of 15 rocks should be identified correctly.

Registration guidelines:

Registration of this event Should be made besides basic registration.
Registration fee to participate in the contest is Rs.200/-(1 Participants).
